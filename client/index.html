Presentation of our PI² project



Context

As part of our Master 1 in FinTech Engineering, we had to carry out an innovative project in teams of 4 students. The different projects were proposed by companies or professors, all falling under the theme of financial engineering, but all being different from each other. The topics were chosen in October and the final reports will be done in April 2020.


Our subject

Our team was particularly attracted by the blockchain technology, we chose to take the topic entitled "Creating and deploying a decentralized application (Dapp) on the blockckhain Ethereum".

The idea is to update the MillionDollarHomePage website that was launched at the beginning of the internet by adding the innovations brought by the ethereum blockchain technology.

This website put on sale 1 million pixels at 1$ per unit, and once purchased the user could put the image he wanted to advertise his website. The problem is that it was not possible to resell the pixel. That's why we wanted to recreate this site with a secondary market system. Indeed the exchanges would be facilitated thanks to a tokenization of the pixels (multi-fungible tokens - ERC721x - you can read the documentation here).
